VIKING BREW

Ingredients

  • The Distilled Fear Of Your Enemies
  • The Sweat Of The Brow Of A Plundering Viking
  • The Essence Of The Evergreen Forests Of Old
  • The Sweet Taste Of Victory Over One's Enemies
  • Some Water

Steps

  1. Begin by wringing the essence of the forest and the sweet taste of victory into a large vat hewn from blessed oak wood
  2. Add the the sweat of plunder and the delicious fear of your enemies into the mixture
  3. Add a dash of water
  4. Commit unto Odin the thrill of your next raid and swear loyalty to the Allfather from now until Valhalla is upon you
  5. Serve neat in a bull horn or skull of your rival
If Odin's Brew Proves Insufficient To Sate Your Appetite, Here Be Stew
