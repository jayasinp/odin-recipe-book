VALHALLA STEW

Ingredients

  • The Freshly Slain Carcass Of A Rare Three Toed Elk
  • You Need None Else

Steps

  1. Prepare a hunting band of trusted men and venture deep into the forest where light fades from thick canopy cover
  2. Spend days tracking and stalking the rare three toed elk
  3. Prepare your traps to ensnare your target
  4. Enter into a life and death struggle with the elk, and when weapons prove meaningless, resort to bare fists and wrestle the creature
  5. Once all hope is fading and victory seems far, call "ODIN" and he will appear
  6. Roast on an open flame and enjoy with comrades. After all you've been through, stew would take too much time to make
